About Us

Dental Services Based on the Highest Standards of Professionalism

RHEINA DENTAL CENTER is located at 3rd Floor HBC Building, Lower Gengeral Luna Road, Baguio City. Rheina Dental Center has earned a reputation as being one of the Dental Center which provide “Dental Services Based on the Highest Standards of Professionalism.”
